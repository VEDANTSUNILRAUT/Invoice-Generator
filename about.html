Our Story

Discover the journey behind Invoice Simple and our mission to simplify invoicing for everyone

Where It All Began

Founded in 2020 by a team of freelancers frustrated with complex invoicing tools, Invoice Simple started as a weekend project. We wanted to create something that actually solved our own problem - getting paid faster without paperwork headaches.

Our initial MVP launched with just the essential features, but quickly gained traction in the creator community who appreciated the no-nonsense approach.

Growth & Evolution

Within a year, we grew to serve over 50,000 users across 80 countries. Our focus remained on simplicity while carefully adding features requested by our community.

In 2022, we introduced mobile apps and multi-currency support, making Invoice Simple accessible to international freelancers and small businesses.

Looking Forward

Today, we're building the future of financial tools for independent creators. Our roadmap includes AI-powered invoice suggestions, expense tracking, and deeper accounting integrations.

We remain committed to our core principle: creating tools that get out of your way so you can focus on your work.

Our Journey
